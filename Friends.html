Best Matchups:

For Introverted Personalities

INTPs (Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Perceiving) are analytical and drawn to the offbeat. ENTJs (Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging) are assertive leaders who like to make sure things run smoothly. They balance each other well, with ENTJs helping bring INTP's ideas into reality

For Extroverted Personalities

ENFPs (Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Perceiving) are enthusiastic explorers who love new ideas. INFJs (Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging) are insightful visionaries. The ENFP's excitement and curiosity complement the INFJ's thoughtfulness and insight. Both value personal growth and making the world a better place

For Introverted Personalities

ISFJs (Introverted, Sensing, Feeling, Judging) value tradition and loyalty. ESFPs (Extraverted, Sensing, Feeling, Perceiving) are spontaneous performers. They balance each other, with ESFPs bringing novelty and ISFJs providing stability



Worst Matchups:

Close Minded

INTPs (Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Perceiving) and ESTJs (Extraverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging) have widely contrasting views on life that can cause friction. INTPs' tendency to analyze deeply can seem inefficient to action-oriented ESTJs

Emotion Disconnections

INFPs are extremely emotionally sensitive, while Thinking-Judging types tend to be very straightforward and less sensitive. The INFP may be attracted to this energy at first but will quickly grow tired of it

Life Experience

The ISFJ and ENTP pairing can be challenging due to their contrasting personalities and approaches to life. ISFJs are reserved, practical, and empathetic, while ENTPs are extraverted, innovative, and thrive on intellectual discussions
